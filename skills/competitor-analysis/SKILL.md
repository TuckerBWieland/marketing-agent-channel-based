## What to Track

### Messaging & Positioning
- Homepage headline and value proposition
- Key differentiators they claim
- Pricing model and anchoring
- Customer proof points (logos, testimonials, case studies)

### Content & Channels
- Blog topics and publishing frequency
- Social platforms and posting cadence
- Email campaigns (sign up for their lists)
- Paid ad copy and landing pages (use Meta Ad Library, Google Ads Transparency)

### Product & Features
- Feature launches and changelog
- Integration partnerships
- Pricing changes

## Research Sources

| Source | What it tells you |
|--------|------------------|
| Their website | Positioning, messaging, pricing |
| Meta Ad Library | Active ad creatives and targeting |
| Google Ads Transparency | Search ad copy |
| [SEMrush/Ahrefs] | Their keyword rankings, backlinks, traffic |
| G2/Capterra reviews | Customer sentiment, strengths, weaknesses |
| LinkedIn | Hiring patterns (what they're investing in) |
| Their blog/newsletter | Content strategy, thought leadership angle |

## Deliverable Formats

### Monthly competitive watch (lightweight)
- 3-5 bullet points on notable competitor moves
- Shared in [Slack channel / team meeting / doc]

### Competitive battlecard (per competitor)
- Their pitch vs. ours
- Their strengths and weaknesses
- Common objections when competing against them
- Talk tracks for sales/marketing

### Quarterly deep dive
- Market positioning map
- Feature comparison matrix
- Content gap analysis
- Recommendations for differentiation
