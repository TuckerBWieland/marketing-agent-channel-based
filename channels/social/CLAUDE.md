## Platform-Specific Rules

### LinkedIn
- **Post length**: 1,300 chars for preview; 3,000 max
- **Best length**: 800-1,200 characters
- **Hashtags**: 3-5 per post
- **Images**: 1200x627px (landscape) or 1080x1080px (square)
- **Tone**: Professional but human. No corporate-speak.

### Twitter/X
- **Post length**: 280 characters (aim for under 240)
- **Threads**: Hook in first tweet. Use for detailed takes.
- **Hashtags**: 1-2 max
- **Images**: 1600x900px (16:9)
- **Tone**: Concise, opinionated, conversational.

### Instagram
- **Caption length**: Lead with the hook in first 125 characters (preview cutoff)
- **Hashtags**: 5-15 in first comment, not caption
- **Image**: 1080x1080px (feed), 1080x1920px (stories/reels)
- **Tone**: Visual-first. Casual, authentic.

## Voice Adjustments by Platform

| Platform | Shift from base voice |
|----------|----------------------|
| LinkedIn | More authoritative, data-driven. Okay to be longer. |
| Twitter/X | Sharper, more concise. Personality forward. |
| Instagram | Warmer, more visual language. Less formal. |

The core voice from root CLAUDE.md always applies — these are adjustments, not overrides.

## Content Mix

Follow the **70/20/10** rule:
- **70%** value content (educate, inform, entertain)
- **20%** shared/curated content (industry news, partner content)
- **10%** promotional (product, offers, CTAs)

## Engagement Rules

- **Response time**: Reply to comments/DMs within [X hours] during business hours
- **Tone for replies**: Match or slightly elevate the commenter's energy
- **Negative comments**: Acknowledge, don't argue. Move to DM for resolution.
- **Never**: Delete negative comments unless they violate community guidelines
- **Escalation**: Flag to [person/team] if comment involves legal, crisis, or executive mention

## UGC & Reposting

- Always credit the original creator
- Get written permission before reposting in paid or owned media
- Never edit UGC to change its meaning
