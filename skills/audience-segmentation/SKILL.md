## Segmentation Methods

### Behavioral (preferred)
- Purchase history, product usage, engagement level
- Most predictive of future behavior

### Demographic
- Role, company size, industry, geography
- Useful for top-of-funnel targeting

### Lifecycle stage
- Prospect > Lead > MQL > SQL > Customer > Advocate
- Determines content type and CTA

## Personalization Rules

- **Minimum**: Address by first name in email, tailor CTA to lifecycle stage
- **Ideal**: Content recommendations based on behavior + segment
- **Never**: Reference data the customer didn't knowingly provide (feels creepy)

## Segment Hygiene

- Review and update segment definitions quarterly
- Remove contacts from segments when criteria no longer met
- Merge overlapping segments — fewer, well-defined segments beat many fuzzy ones
